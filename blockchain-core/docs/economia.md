Moral Money – Economic Layer

The economic layer of the ecosystem records assets, contributions and infrastructure inside the community.

Blockchain technology is used to ensure transparency and verifiable records.

Assets, investments and infrastructure contributions can be registered through the system.

The economic layer supports internal accounting of value generated by projects and contributions.

The system may include tokens or digital assets that represent participation in productive activity or infrastructure development.

The goal is to create a transparent economic environment where contributions are recorded and recognized without allowing financial capital to dominate governance.
