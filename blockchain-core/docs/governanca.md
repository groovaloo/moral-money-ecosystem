Moral Money – Governance Model

The governance system is based on competence rather than permanent political roles.

There is no permanent governing class within the system.

When a decision must be made in a specific domain, the system selects the individuals with the highest reputation in that domain.

The five most reputable participants are temporarily grouped into a decision council.

This council evaluates the situation and takes the necessary decision. After the decision is completed, the council dissolves automatically.

This mechanism ensures that governance authority emerges dynamically from demonstrated competence rather than from permanent power structures.
